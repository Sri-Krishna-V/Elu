Welcome to Elu ✿

Elu is an AI-powered reading assistant designed to make web content more accessible for everyone — especially for those with dyslexia, ADHD, and cognitive differences.

Here's what Elu can do for you:

AI Simplify

On-device AI rewrites complex text into clearer language

Optimize For

Tune AI for clarity, visual structure, or reading flow

Smart Chunks

Break long articles into bite-sized, manageable pieces

Focus Mode

Dim distractions and play ambient sounds for concentration

Bionic Reading

Bold word beginnings to guide your eyes faster

Read Aloud

High-quality text-to-speech with speed & voice control

OpenDyslexic

Switch to a typeface designed for easier reading

Themes & Spacing

Customise colours and text spacing to reduce strain

🤖 On-device AI Elu downloads a small AI model the first time you simplify text. This runs entirely on your device — nothing is sent to the cloud.

Choose Your Profile

Select the profile that best fits your reading needs. You can always change this later from the popup.

⚡ AI Model Selection

💡 Qwen 2.5: Fastest model, uses minimal VRAM. Great for older GPUs.
💎 SmolLM2: Higher quality responses, requires more VRAM and processing time.

Here are some keyboard shortcuts to get you started quickly:

Alt+S Simplify the current page with AI
Alt+F Toggle Focus Mode
Alt+R Read aloud (text-to-speech)
💡 Quick tip Click the Elu ✿ icon in your browser toolbar to open the popup and access all features. Your profile and settings are saved automatically and sync across devices.
